# Federal Survey Concept Mapping Study - Report Writing Plan

## Report Title
**"AI-Assisted Concept Mapping of Federal Survey Questions: A Scalable Approach to Survey Taxonomy Analysis"**

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: U.S. Census Bureau leadership and statisticians
- **Secondary**: Federal statistical agencies, academic researchers

## Key Messages
1. **Scalability**: LLM-based approach processed 6,987 questions in hours vs. hundreds of hours manually
2. **Accuracy**: 99.5% successful categorization with dual-model validation
3. **Actionable**: Identified consolidation opportunities, coverage gaps, and redundancies
4. **Reproducible**: Open methodology applicable to other survey systems
